03 Affinity Diagramming

After the generation of our 64 initial design ideas we did affinity diagramming to determine the underlying needs that our ideas were addressing. We came up with the following 6 needs:

  • Awareness
  • Appreciation
  • Communication/ Coordination
  • Favors
  • Responsibility
  • Social
