Script Feedback

-1st scene - instead of seeing into another room at party; show someone bar crawling and can see party pics on mobile device

-Show privacy by having a couple walk into a room and saying "off the record" -> maybe have this play into the morning after? should a roommate come down with a girl? or should they be looking thru pics and say how come no pics are in this room?

-Cake throwing scene is not compelling; show a wii accident instead!

-Should have overlays to explain the communication between the frames and cameras

-Multiple cameras and one frame in each room -> maybe can show that pics get taken from diff angles (like when someone says SAVE)

-Make the script more believable

-Maybe shouldn't just show pics getting taken at a party, but could show a picture getting taken the morning after when the roommates are around the TV hanging out and watching

-Video could end with that new picture on the wall
