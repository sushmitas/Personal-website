02 Main Themes

There are a couple of main themes that PicturePal touches upon. They are house pride, awareness and memory.

House Pride: Our product accentuates the social bonds between roommates and allows them to show it to the world. Roommates can showcase how well they get along with the pictures displayed on their walls. Visitors see the pictures that appear in the frames and are able to glean insight into the social activites as well as the closeness of the roommates.
Awareness: PicturePal allows roommates to see when an event is going on at home and hurry back to join in on the fun. They can also use it to keep aware of past events and their details. For example: If the roommates want to throw a Halloween party they can view last year’s party pictures so they can be sure to mix it up and do things a bit differently this year.
Memory: The system allows the roommates to view pictures of their past together and reminisce, which enriches the bond between the roommates.
