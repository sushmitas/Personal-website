04 Concept Validation

Using the 6 needs we drew out of our affinity diagramming we made the following illustrations which we used for concept validation.

 

Favors

WishList: Ann wishes she had time to go out and buy herself ice-cream. Jenny is in the supermarket when she notices the wish. She buys Ann some ice cream.

 

DinnerBell: Jane made dinner. She cooked too much food. She rings the bell to notify her roommates that they can grab a plate.

 

Appreciation

ThankYouNote: Bob does the dishes. Dave leaves a thank you note for Bob above the kitchen sink.

 

Kudos: Bob does the dishes. Dave sees this, and gives Bob some Kudos for his hard work.

 

Awareness

RoomLight: Jenny is feeling blue. Her room turns blue. Dan sees this, and brings her a cup of coffee.

 

NoiseMinder: Dan is playing games on his PC. The system sees that Dave is sleeping, and suggests Dan to put the volume down.

 

Communication/Coordination

DoorChat: Dan, Adam and Dave are going bowling. Jenny can’t come. She’s happy that her roommates don’t just barge in with these ideas.

 

ShowerViz: Rick has a presentation at 8AM. He looks at the ShowerViz to find when his roommates usually take a shower, so that he won’t have to wait in the morning.

 

Social

KodakMoments: Dan and Bob are having fun in the living room. Bob makes a joke. A camera captures the “Kodak moment”

 

HouseRobot: Jenny baked some cookies. She lets the house robot present them to her roommates.

 

HouseHeroes: There’s a comic book about the roommates that shows upcoming events. Jane reads that they will be cleaning this week, and that Dan has an important test.

 

Responsibility

LocationSticks: Just when he is about to leave, Dan spots a pile of rubbish on the floor. He quickly throws a LocationStick at it. A panel at the door spots the places that need cleaning.

 

Kill the DirtPet: Oh no! The DirtPet respawned again and is feeding on the loud music and the dirty dishes! The roommates team up to clean the house and kill the DirtPet.

 

CollectThemAll: Dave is doing the dishes. He now collected 5 chores on the chore-board! That means his friends treat him to a day at the theme park.
