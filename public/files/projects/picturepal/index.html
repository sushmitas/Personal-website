PicturePal



Summary


PicturePal is an intelligent agent that connects to cameras around the house which automatically take pictures. The frame, which hangs on the wall, displays pictures showing off the roommates social activities, allowing the roommates to look back and reminisce. It also facilitates awareness of events both past and present. PicturePal is the system that enables roommates who are friends to become friends who are roommates.
