Feedback on Rough Draft Video:

-We explain too much and should maybe just show the features in some places.

-Get a wider shot of the room when looking over the frame. Need to see the frame next to the TV to see that it's in the same room.

-Narrator talks too fast - hard to understand. Have to change "Hey dude, gimme a kiss" if we keep the narrator because the voices sound the same.

-Rephotograph Jack walking up to an apartment building, rather than a house.

-Instead of an abstract "coolest pic" have it with a real frame and a real picture.

-Fix the frame as a controller - make it have controls instead of just showing the picture.

-Don't need the camera icon when the frame is taking a picture - just add snapping sound or some indicator.

-Focus in on Margaret saying "Wait" and then zoom out to show the group to show that she's the one talking.

-Make the TV pics from halloween photos to make it distinctive that it's a separate event.

-Make Brad at a coffee shop when he gets the notification - so that it doesn't just look like he's heading home anyways.

-Get an older voice as the narrator.

-1st music track - change this - too much like party dance music.

-Last music track - pick a slower track.
