02 Interview Questions

Do you have any roommates? -or- Have you had any roommates in the past? How long ago?
Are you friends with him/her?
Are there others in the apartment/house/dorm room besides the two of you?
How long have you been rooming together for?
Are you friends with your roommate?

Do you have "roles"? (explicit or implied)

Have you made arrangements about things?
- lifestyles
- cleaning
- noise
- use of facilities
- buying things for the home
- financial issues
Do you comply to these arrangements?
How about your roommate?

Are there ever any issues between the two of you?
What are the issues about?
- lifestyles
- cleaning
- noise
- use of facilities
- buying things for the home
- financial issues
How, if at all, do you reconcile them?

Does your roommate ever do anything that pleases you?
What is the most recent thing he/she did for you and how did it please you?
Did you give him/her thanks or feedback that you appreciated it?

Do you ever do anything that pleased your roommate?
What was the last thing you did for him/her?
Was there anything that motivated you to do this?

Is there anything you particularly dislike about having a roommate? What is it?
Is there anything you particularly like? What is it?

What do you look for when you look for a roommate?
Why?
