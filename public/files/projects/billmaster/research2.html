02 Our Users

We focused on people who are experts in this domain - money micromanagers who are willing to put the time in to manage their finances. By studying and understanding the behavior and motivations of people who have perfected the notion of being in control of their money, we felt we could better design a system that provides this level of control to others as well.
IID 2007 . Human-Computer Interaction Institute . Carnegie Mellon University
