03 Competitive Analysis

There are many billpaying systems on the market, and we did not want to create just another billpaying service with redundant features. Therefore, it was necessary to canvass the web and question our users to find out which software and websites people use and why. After compiling a list of the relevant services, we sat down to review them. We inspected Quicken, Microsoft Money, American Express, Citibank, Visa and DiscoverCard.
