BillMaster Technologies

Linking expenses to experiences


Summary

We propose a new format for an online credit card statement that provides a more meaningful presentation of the information users need when paying a bill.

Our system groups expenses into representative categories, with a level of granularity that corresponds to the user and the expenses themselves. Users can visualize their current expenses and overall spending trends, in the form of pie charts and line graphs. The system also makes a concrete tie between expenses and experiences in users' lives. Join us in a tour through our process from conception to actualization.
