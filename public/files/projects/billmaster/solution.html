01 Overview of BillMaster

We designed a service to empower users with an easy way to be aware of their spendings. We tie expenses to experiences.

The BillMaster system integrates the user's calendar to extract and associate meaningful information with a purchase rather than just the amount. Purchases that do not fall into the user's regular spending patterns are associated with an event or experience in the user's life. We flag these items on the statement to support users' current mental model of scanning through a statement for extraordinary purchases.

The system leverages knowledge of the user's payment history and provides users visualizations of how their spendings have changed over time. Users can also compare their current spendings with those in the past. These trends and comparisons may help influence the users' spending purchases in the future.

Our statements organize purchases into categories so users can have a better sense of how they spend their money during the course of the month. This provides users better control over their spending purchases, and allows them to review their expenses in a more meaningful way than the standard statement list.

We believe in emphasizing experiences in our system so that users can make an association between the purchase and the value it added to their experience.
