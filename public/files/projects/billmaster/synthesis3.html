03 Mental Model

We designed a system to address four main questions that users are likely to ask when visiting a billpaying website:

-How can I pay my bill? (of course)
-What did I spend money on this month?
-Have I spent more than I usually do?
-What did I spend in previous months?

We created a diagram to represent the user's mental model when addressing these questions on the website.
